About Us

Hometown resort located in a peaceful surrounding, close to the heart of its world heritage and a warm smile of local community. At Hometown resort, Thai hospitality and grace make staying in Sukhothai a leisurely and enjoyable past time.

The story of Hometown Resort begins in the early 2000s, when a visionary Thai entrepreneur named Chaiya Pongphan, originally from Sukhothai, returned to his hometown after spending several years abroad. While living in cities like Bangkok and Chiang Mai, he found himself drawn to the rich history and tranquil landscapes of Sukhothai, the former capital of the Kingdom of Sukhothai (1238–1438). The city, recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site, was famous for its ancient ruins, the serene beauty of its lakes, and its historic temples, such as Wat Mahathat, which housed the remains of the Kingdom's founders.

Chaiya, inspired by the peace and timeless beauty of his ancestral land, envisioned creating a place where visitors could experience the calm and culture of Sukhothai in an intimate, luxurious setting. His goal was to build a resort that would blend modern comforts with the rich heritage and natural surroundings of the region.

A Legacy of Thai Heritage and Hospitality

By 2020, Hometown Resort had become one of the top resorts in Sukhothai, attracting visitors from around the world who were looking for an authentic Thai experience in a serene and historic setting. Over the years, Chaiya’s vision had come to life—Hometown Resort was not just a place to stay, but a place where guests felt like part of a larger community, connected to the heritage and beauty of the land.

As the resort celebrated its 15th anniversary in 2022, it continued to evolve, with new initiatives focused on preserving the environment and further promoting local culture. The resort had become a model for sustainable tourism in Thailand, with initiatives aimed at protecting the delicate ecosystem surrounding the Sukhothai Historical Park.

The legacy of Hometown Resort is rooted in its founder's love for his homeland and his commitment to creating a place where guests could experience the very best of Sukhothai—its culture, its history, and its breathtaking natural beauty. Chaiya’s passion for preserving the traditions of his ancestors and offering a sanctuary of peace and serenity to visitors made Hometown Resort not just a destination, but a home away from home.

Today: A Pillar of Thai Hospitality

As of 2024, Hometown Resort continues to thrive, offering guests a peaceful retreat in the heart of Thailand’s ancient capital. Its dedication to sustainable tourism, community engagement, and cultural preservation has made it a beloved landmark in Sukhothai and a symbol of the harmony that can exist between nature, heritage, and modern luxury.
